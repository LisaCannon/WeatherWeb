Latitude vs Temperature

The results of the study suggest that there is a strong quadratic relationship between temperature and latitude. Temperatures are hotter near the center of the globe and cooler towards the poles. The peak of this trend is not at the equator, as predicted, but instead about 15° north of the equator.
